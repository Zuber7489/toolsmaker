Why use a Character Counter?

Every social media platform has different limits for their posts, bios, and titles. Exceeding these limits can result in your message being cut off or your post failing to publish. Our **free online character counter** helps you draft your content to fit perfectly within these constraints.

Instagram Bio Limit

Instagram bios are limited to 150 characters. Use this space wisely to convey who you are and what you do.

Twitter / X Character Limit

Standard tweets are limited to 280 characters. Blue subscribers may have more, but brevity is key on this platform.

How to check Character Limits?

Different social platforms have different limits. Twitter allows 280 characters, Instagram captions allow 2200, and LinkedIn posts allow 3000. Our tool tracks these in real-time as you type, ensuring you never go over the limit.
