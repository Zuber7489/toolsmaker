Everything you need to know about QR Codes

QR (Quick Response) codes are everywhere. From restaurant menus to digital business cards, they provide a quick, scannable way to share information. Our tool lets you generate fully custom QR codes for free without any limitations.

Why use our generator?

  • No Expiry: Static codes work forever.
  • High Quality: Crisp PNG output.
  • Privacy: 100% Client-side generation.

Pro Tip

When choosing colors, always ensure there is a high contrast between your background (light) and foreground (dark). If the contrast is too low, some cameras might struggle to scan the code.

Recommended: B&W or similar high contrast.
