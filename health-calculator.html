How to calculate BMI & BMR?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a simple calculation using a person's height and weight. The formula is BMI = kg/m², where kg is a person's weight in kilograms and m² is their height in metres squared.

Daily Calorie Needs (BMR)

Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) is the number of calories your body burns while at rest to maintain vital functions like breathing and circulation. Knowing your BMR helps you plan your diet for weight loss, maintenance, or muscle gain.
