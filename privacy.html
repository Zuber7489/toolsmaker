Privacy Policy

At FreeProducts24, we take your privacy extremely seriously. Unlike many other tool websites, we have designed our tools to be **Client-Side First**.

1. Data Collection

The majority of the tools on FreeProducts24 (like the Image Compressor, JSON Formatter, and Password Generator) process data exclusively in your web browser. We do not upload, store, or see the data you input into these tools.

2. Local Storage

Some tools may use browser 'Local Storage' to save your preferences (like calculation history or theme settings) so they are available when you revisit. This data stays on your machine and is never shared with us.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services like Google Analytics or Search Console to monitor site performance and traffic. these services may use cookies to collect standard logging info. This is used solely to improve our user experience.

4. Cookies

Cookies are small files placed on your device to help analyze web traffic. You can choose to accept or decline cookies through your browser settings.

5. Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. Because we do not store your personal tool data on our servers, there is no risk of your processed data being leaked from our end.

By using FreeProducts24, you agree to this simple and transparent privacy policy. Last updated: 2024.
