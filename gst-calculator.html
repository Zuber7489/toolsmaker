GST Calculation for India (2024)

The Goods and Services Tax (GST) is an indirect tax used in India on the supply of goods and services. Our online GST calculator helps you find the Net Price, GST Amount, and Gross Price instantly. Whether you are a business owner, accountant, or a shopper, this tool simplifies tax planning.

GST Components: CGST, SGST, IGST

  • CGST & SGST: Applicable for intra-state (within the same state) transactions. Each tier takes half of the total GST rate.
  • IGST: Applicable for inter-state (between two different states) transactions.
