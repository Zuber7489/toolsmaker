Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need a Rent Receipt?

If you are a salaried employee claiming HRA (House Rent Allowance) exemption, you must submit rent receipts to your employer as proof of rent payment. This is mandatory for claiming tax benefits.

Is Landlord's PAN mandatory?

The Landlord's PAN card is mandatory if your annual rent payment exceeds ₹1,00,000 (approx. ₹8,333 per month). For amounts below this, PAN is not strictly required but recommended.

Can I generate monthly or quarterly receipts?

Yes! You can generate a single receipt for a full year, quarterly, or monthly payments. Just adjust the "From Date" and "To Date" and the "Rent Amount" accordingly (e.g., multiply monthly rent by 3 for quarterly).
