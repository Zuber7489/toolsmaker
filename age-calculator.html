About This Age Calculator

Calculating your age manually can be tricky due to leap years and varying month lengths (28, 30, or 31 days). This free online Age Calculator does the math instantly for you.

Just enter your date of birth, and we'll calculate exactly how many years, months, and days you've been alive. It's perfect for verifying age for forms, planning birthdays, or satisfying your curiosity.
