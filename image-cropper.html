How to Crop Images

  1. Upload: Click "Upload" or drag an image onto the canvas.
  2. Adjust: Drag the corners of the crop box to select your area.
  3. Fine-tune: Use zoom, rotate, and flip tools for improved composition.
  4. Set Ratio: Choose a preset (like 1:1 for Instagram) or Free crop.
  5. Download: Click "Crop & Download" to save your edited image.
