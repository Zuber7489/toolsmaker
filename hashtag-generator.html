How to Use Hashtags effectively?

Using hashtags is one of the easiest ways to get your posts seen by people who don't follow you yet. However, simply using 30 random tags won't work. You need to mix popular hashtags (1M+ posts) with niche-specific ones (10k-50k posts) to stand a chance of ranking on the explore page.

  • Use between 3 to 15 relevant hashtags per post.
  • Hide tags in the first comment to keep the caption clean.
  • Vary your tag sets for different posts to avoid being flagged as spam.
  • Analyze which tags bring you the most engagement.
