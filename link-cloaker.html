Why Cloak Your Affiliate Links?

1. Build Trust & Branding

Users are 3x more likely to click on a link that looks like your brand (e.g., yoursite.com/recommends/product) than a random messy URL full of tracking IDs.

2. Avoid Link Hijacking

Some malicious browser extensions can swap your affiliate IDs with their own if the link is recognizable. Cloaked links hide the final destination until the user clicks.

3. Easier Link Management

If an affiliate program changes its links, you only need to update the redirect file once. You don't have to edit 100 different blog posts or social captions.

4. Better CTR on Social Media

Short, readable links perform significantly better on platforms like Twitter, Instagram, and YouTube where space is limited and aesthetics matter.
